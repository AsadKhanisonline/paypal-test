Request Account Deletion

If you want to delete your account and associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the paypal account.
  2. Open the 'Settings' menu by clicking the icon near 'Log out'.
  3. Under 'Account options', click the 'Close your account' link..
  4. Click the 'Close account' button..
    5. Once it's gone, it's gone.
